You have friends all over the world
You just haven‘t met them yet

Make international
friends, and experience
the world as a global family

Tribevibe is a diverse and vibrant community of people that
connect online, and meet offline.It‘s not for evening hangouts
easily forgotten, but for creating deep and meaningful
connections, to allow genuine expressions, and to ingrain a sense of belonging.

Made for travellers, expats, digital nomads, flâneurs,
adventurers, and curious people, who want to expand their
global friendships as they saunter the earth.
We are still small, but we are hungry to make new friends, whether in our
hometown or a faraway land.

Designed for
offline experiences

Real friends are not requests.
They happen, but outside, not in front
of a flickering screen.
Tribevibe is designed with simplicity in mind,
to create a feeling of calmness, and to be
remembered not by its fancy features,
but by your genuine offline experiences.

Are you a Tribeviber?

Typical values that our community entertains.

  • You embrace the differences in cultures, traditions, and beliefs.
  • You give more than take, and you understand that giving makes you richer.
  • You are not seeking a vacation so much as an immersion.
  • You are aware that the journey is as important as the destination.
  • You understand the difference between a tourist, and a traveller.
  • You strive to minimise your ecological travel footprint.
  • You are a perennial seeker of meaning and a life-long-learner.
